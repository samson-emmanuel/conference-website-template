About

Expanding growth beyond limit is all about elevating the principles of innovation and excellence to unprecedented levels. By closely monitoring industry trends and harnessing emerging technologies, we aim to break through conventional barriers that restrict growth. The theme strives to inspire us to envision ambitious goals and cultivate an environment where transformative ideas are not just welcomed but actively pursued. The ultimate aim is to implement cutting-edge solutions that enhance operational efficiency, elevate customer satisfaction, and secure a leading position in the marketplace.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the theme of the conference?
The theme of the conference is "Driving Innovation and Excellence: Expanding Growth Beyond Limit."
Where is the conference being held?
The conference is a residential event hosted at the Lagos Continental Hotel.
Who are the keynote speakers?
The keynote speakers include industry leaders and innovators. Check the speakers' section for more details.
What is the hotel check-in time?
Delegates are expected to arrive at the Lagos Continental Hotel on Wednesday, 29 January 2025, between 14:00 and 15:30 for registration and check-in. It is recommended to arrive on time to complete check-in/registration.
What is the theme of the conference?
The theme of the conference is "Driving Innovation and Excellence: Expanding Growth Beyond Limit."
When I arrive at the hotel location, where do I check in?
Upon your arrival at The Lagos Continental Hotel, please proceed to the Soul Lounge on the ground floor for check-in.
How do I get my conference shirts?
For Lagos-based delegates, conference shirts will be distributed at the Ikoyi head office, with further details to follow shortly. Delegates traveling from outside Lagos will receive their shirts upon arrival at the conference venue.
Where will Delegate Registration be done?
Upon arrival at the hotel, please proceed to the registration desk on the third floor. You will see the appropriate signage.
Will transport arrangement be made for delegates outside Lagos?
Yes. Transport arrangement will be made for outside-Lagos delegates.
