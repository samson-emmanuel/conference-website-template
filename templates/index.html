About

The 2026 Leadership Conference is designed to set the tone, discipline, and execution mindset for a year defined by exceptionally demanding targets. Having delivered last year’s ambitious commitments, the organization is now entering a phase where excellence must become repeatable, company-wide, and leader-led.

Expected outcomes include:
  • Clear organization ambition and priorities
  • Explicit functional commitments to deliver on ambitions
  • strengthened cross-functional trust and alignment
  • a leadership team prepared to deliver consistently at an exceptional level

WELCOME TO THE 2026
LAFARGE AFRICA
LEADERSHIP CONFERENCE

As leaders, your insights and expertise are critical in steering the organization towards its strategic goals. Together, we’ll explore new ideas, tackle challenges, and chart a path toward even greater success. Thank you for being here and and co- creating the future with us.
